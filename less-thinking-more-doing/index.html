Less Thinking; More Doing

May 20th 2023 By David Gorski

I am cursed with the pathological trait of intense over analysis. I have many ideas. But for an idea of mine to the surface and become 'real', it must complete a mental obstacle course equivalent to a medieval gauntlet.

After a lot of thinking (ironically) I have decided go act more. One such act is the creation of this blog and first blog post.

I am starting with stream-of-consiouness, low-effort writing. As I get more comfortable I hope to unload more serious and more fun topics. Signing off for the first time. DG.
